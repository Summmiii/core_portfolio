    XU

    Wenyong Xu, born in 1969 at Ningbo, Zhejiang.(right)

    Ying Zhang, born in 1972 at Shaoxing, Zhejiang.(left)

    Xu, born in 1969 in Ningbo, Zhejiang. He received his Batchelor of Finance Degree at Zhejiang University at age of 23.
    Zhang, born in 1972 in Shaoxing, Zhejiang, received her Batchelor of English Degree at Zhejiang Wanli College at age of 20.
    With a common interest in finance and economics, Xu and Zhang both entered the Bank of China after graduation. This Chinese couple met each other with full of love and, luckily, stepped into the palace of marriage in 1997 with faith and persistence.
    Zhang’s first pregnancy ended up with abortion due to her unaware medicine intake; soon, Zhang got her second pregnancy and gave birth to a female, weighed 3.25kg, on July 7th, 1999, named Shuming.
    Unlike common Chinese parents, Xu and Zhang followed the education methodology that kids should be allowed to choose their future.
    With full supports and freedom, Shuming decided to follow her own passion in art and design and go to Parsons School of Design as a Fashion Design major;
    But soon she realized her real passion and strength in graphics and interactions, hence she dropped from Fashion and declared her major as a Communication and Interaction Design.
